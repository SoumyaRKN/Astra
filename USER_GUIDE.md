## 1. Chat Interface

### 1.1 Basic Text Chat

**How it works:**

1. Launch Personal AI Assistant in browser
2. Click the **chat input field** at the bottom
3. Type your message
4. Press **Enter** or click **Send** button
5. Wait for response (usually 3-10 seconds)
6. Read response and continue conversation

**Example:**

```
You:     "What's the capital of Japan?"
AI:      "The capital of Japan is Tokyo. It's the largest metropolitan 
          area in the world and has been the seat of government 
          since 1868."

You:     "Tell me about its history"
AI:      "Tokyo was originally known as Edo. It became the capital 
          when the Tokugawa shogunate moved its seat of power there..."
```

## 2. Voice Chat

### 2.1 Enable Voice

**First-time setup:**

1. Go to **Settings** → **Voice**
2. Grant browser microphone permission
   - Click 🔒 icon in address bar
   - Select "Site Settings"
   - Microphone: **Allow**
3. Select your preferred voice
4. Click **Test** to hear sample

### 2.2 Voice Chat Steps

**To use voice:**

1. In chat interface, click 🎤 **Microphone** button
2. Red indicator shows recording is active
3. Speak naturally into microphone
4. When done, click 🎤 again to stop
5. Wait for transcription (2-5 seconds)
6. Your message appears as text
7. AI generates response and speaks it aloud
8. Avatar animates while speaking (if enabled)

**Example:**

```
1. Click microphone
2. Say: "What's the recipe for chocolate cake?"
3. Transcribed text appears: "What's the recipe for chocolate cake?"
4. AI responds with recipe, spoken aloud
5. Avatar shows mouth movements matching speech
```

### 2.3 Voice Settings

**Text-to-Speech (TTS) Settings:**

- **Voice:** Female, Male, Neutral variants
- **Speed:** 0.5x (slow) to 2.0x (fast) - default 1.0x
- **Pitch:** Adjust tone of voice (-50 to +50)
- **Volume:** Output loudness (0-100%)
- **Test:** Listen to sample before saving

**Speech-to-Text (STT) Settings:**

- **Language:** Auto-detect or specify (English, Spanish, etc.)
- **Microphone:** Select input device
- **Sensitivity:** Threshold for speech detection
- **Echo Cancellation:** Reduce background noise

### 2.4 Continuous Voice Chat

**For hands-free conversation:**

1. Enable "Continuous Voice Mode" in settings
2. Say your message
3. AI responds automatically
4. AI's response automatically starts recording for next message
5. Keep talking naturally
6. Click anywhere to stop

## 5. Generate Images

### 5.1 Create Image

**Steps:**

1. Click **Gallery** in sidebar
2. Click **"Create New"** button
3. Select **"Generate Image"**
4. Enter detailed prompt describing what you want
5. Click **"Generate"** button
6. Wait 30-120 seconds (depends on system)
7. View generated image
8. Save to gallery or download

**Example prompts:**

```
✓ "A serene mountain landscape at sunset, oil painting style, 
   highly detailed, professional quality"

✓ "A cozy coffee shop interior with warm lighting, customers reading, 
   autumn atmosphere, photorealistic"

✓ "A futuristic city with flying cars, neon lights, cyberpunk aesthetic, 
   highly detailed digital art"

✗ "A cat" (too vague)
✗ "Something interesting" (unclear)
```

### 5.2 Image Generation Settings

**Before generating:**

- **Model:** Choose between different AI models (varies quality/speed)
- **Quality:** Draft (fast), Standard, Premium (slower but better)
- **Aspect Ratio:** Square (1:1), Widescreen (16:9), Portrait (9:16)
- **Style:** Photorealistic, Painting, Cartoon, 3D Render, etc.
- **Guidance:** 7.5 (creative) to 15 (strict matching of prompt)

### 5.3 Regenerate & Refine

**If you don't like result:**

- **Regenerate:** Same prompt, new image
- **Upscale:** Increase resolution of generated image
- **Variation:** Similar to original but different

**Refining:**

1. Current image shown
2. Enter new prompt or modification
3. Click "Refine"
4. New image generated with both old and new context

### 5.4 Image Gallery

**View your images:**

**Image actions:**

## 9. Tips for Better Results

### 9.1 Chat Tips

**Get Better Responses:**

1. **Be specific:** "How do I make sourdough bread?" (not "How to bake?")
2. **Provide context:** "I'm learning Python for data science..." (background helps)
3. **Ask follow-ups:** "Explain that more simply" or "Can you give an example?"
4. **Clarify terms:** "When you said 'framework', do you mean Django?"
5. **Request format:** "Use bullet points" or "Give a step-by-step guide"

**Good Questions:**

- "What are the top 3 best practices for X?"
- "Can you explain X in simple terms for a beginner?"
- "Compare X and Y - what are pros and cons of each?"
- "Create a step-by-step guide to..."
- "What would happen if...?"

**Poor Questions:**

- "Tell me everything about X" (too vague)
- "Why?" (without context)
- "Fix this" (without showing code)
- Questions with multiple unrelated topics at once

### 9.2 Voice Tips

- **Speak clearly:** Enunciate words properly
- **Pause between thoughts:** Let AI process each part
- **Good microphone:** USB microphones work better than built-in mics
- **Quiet environment:** Minimize background noise
- **Natural pace:** Don't rush or speak too slowly

### 9.3 Image Generation Tips

**Effective prompts include:**

1. **What:** What is in the image?
   - "A sunset over mountains"
   - "Portrait of a woman"

2. **Style:** Artistic style
   - "Oil painting"
   - "Photorealistic photograph"
   - "Digital art"
   - "Watercolor"

3. **Mood:** Atmosphere or emotion
   - "Peaceful and serene"
   - "Vibrant and energetic"
   - "Dark and mysterious"

4. **Quality:** Professional attributes
   - "Highly detailed"
   - "Professional photography"
   - "4K resolution"
   - "Award-winning"

5. **Lighting:** How it's lit
   - "Golden hour lighting"
   - "Dramatic shadows"
   - "Soft diffused light"

**Example good prompt:**

```
"A serene Japanese garden with a koi pond, stone lanterns, 
maple trees in autumn colors, morning mist rising, oil painting 
style, highly detailed, professional quality, golden hour lighting"
```

### 9.4 Video Tips

- **Clear descriptions:** More specific = better results
- **Reasonable duration:** 5-15 seconds usually best
- **Style preferences:** Mention if you want realistic or animated
- **Action verbs:** Use dynamic words (flying, dancing, transforming)

## 10. Troubleshooting

### Issue: AI Takes Too Long to Respond

**Cause:** CPU-limited without GPU

**Solutions:**

1. Close other applications
2. Wait longer (first response is slower)
3. Try smaller model (can change in settings)
4. Use GPU if available (install CUDA)

### Issue: Microphone Not Working

**Solutions:**

1. Check browser permissions (see Voice Chat section)
2. Test microphone in system settings
3. Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
4. Restart browser
5. Check microphone is not muted

### Issue: Avatar Looks Frozen

**Solution:**

- Restart frontend: `docker compose restart frontend`
- Disable and re-enable avatar
- Use different avatar style

### Issue: Can't Generate Images

**Cause:** GPU out of memory

**Solutions:**

1. Reduce image resolution
2. Use "Draft" quality instead of "Premium"
3. Restart services to clear memory
4. Close other applications

### Issue: Lost Conversations

**Solution:**

```
If closed improperly:
1. Check sidebar "Recently Deleted"
2. Go to Settings → Privacy → Export Data
3. Download backup file
4. Contact support with backup
```
